abstract: This article studies the Aharonov-Bohm effect within phase-space and Segal-Bargmann formulations of quantum mechanics. It develops a consistent description of the effect using quasiprobability methods and connects these descriptions to the standard configuration-space formalism. The work also analyzes interference signatures in the evolution of Wigner functions and highlights the intrinsically quantum nature of the phenomenon.
